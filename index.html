What is rowing?

It is a sport that is practiced on the water. The boats are very thin and you can be alone or 8 in the boat.

It exists 2 type of boat.

  • scull
  • row
Scull
2 oars

The rower has two oars in his hands. It is easier to row and have balance

Row
one oar

The rower has only one oar. Balancing becomes more complicated.
